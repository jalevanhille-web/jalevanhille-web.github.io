Lunar Base

Briefing

How to Play

Goal: Survive 100 days and grow your crew to 20+. Each game day = 2 real seconds.

Resources: Crew use oxygen and water every day. Power drives every module — solar arrays only generate during sunlight, and lunar nights last 3 days. Materials build modules. Research unlocks tech.

What to do first: Build more habitats to grow your crew (you start at the housing cap), then a Mining Rig for material income or a Research Lab to unlock tech. Keep oxygen and water above 50 — if either hits zero for 3 days, your crew dies.

Curveballs: Meteors, dust storms, malfunctions. Build redundantly. Research Nuclear Battery early if you want to survive long nights.
