About

I'm a Finance student at BYU's Marriott School of Business focused on investment banking and growth equity. Before college I served a two-year mission in Boise, Idaho, working in Spanish among the Latino community. Since starting at BYU I've worked as a research analyst across venture capital, transaction liability, and growth equity — most recently at Resonance Partners, where I diligence lower-middle-market acquisition targets.

My deeper interest is in exponential technologies — AI, biotech, energy, space — and in the kind of investing that funds them at the frontier. The companies built over the next decade will shape what abundance looks like for humanity, and I want to help direct capital toward the ones doing it well.
