I · The Premise

Stillness, on purpose.

Last summer, in a difficult semester, I found I needed the scriptures to do more than sit in a corner of my day. I had been reading thirty minutes each morning; it wasn’t enough. I wanted the Lord’s words to follow me — into the library, into the walk home, into the hours I would otherwise be scrolling.

On YouTube I found what I was looking for: meditative readings paired with quiet visuals and sincere narration, with millions of views. But nearly all of them were from the Bible. There was nothing like it for the Book of Mormon, the Doctrine and Covenants, or the Pearl of Great Price. The scriptures of the Restoration had no voice in that space.

So I started Restoration Scriptures. The early videos were recorded on my mom’s handheld audio recorder; more recently I’ve been booking the audio rooms at BYU. The channel is small — forty-nine subscribers at the time of writing — but I believe these scriptures carry a combined witness of Jesus Christ that the world hasn’t heard enough of. I’d like to help put that witness where people are already looking for stillness.

— Leo

II · The Channel, So Far

Four videos, four meditations.

Each video pulls together scripture from across the Restoration canon around a single theme. The list below is every video I’ve published, in order, with the passages read in each.

  1. God’s Words in Grief

    A Scripture Reading for the Brokenhearted. Released August 2025. For anyone overwhelmed by grief or by life’s unfairness.

    • 2 Nephi 4 — the Psalm of Nephi
    • Doctrine & Covenants 121 — Joseph Smith’s prayer in Liberty Jail
    • Doctrine & Covenants 122 — the Lord’s answer

  2. There Is Purpose to Your Pain

    Healing Scripture Meditation. Released August 2025. Built around Ezekiel 22:21 — “As silver is melted in the midst of the furnace, so shall ye be melted in the midst thereof.”

    • Isaiah 48:10–11
    • 2 Corinthians 4:15–18
    • Ezekiel 22:18–22
    • Mosiah 23:21–22 & 24:13–17
    • Alma 14:8–13
    • Doctrine & Covenants 58:2–4

  3. The Words of Jesus Christ, Part I

    Easter Scripture Meditation. Released March 2026. The first half of a two-part Easter series, organized around two themes: “I am in your midst” and “I have suffered for you.”

    • Doctrine & Covenants 6, 19, 29, 45, 84, 121, 122
    • Mosiah 24:13–14
    • 3 Nephi 11:10–14

  4. The Words of Jesus Christ, Part II

    A 30-minute Easter meditation. Released April 2026. The most recent upload. Three themes, drawn from the voice of Christ across the Restoration scriptures: Fear not · Come unto me · My kindness shall not depart.

    • Doctrine & Covenants 6, 38, 50, 61, 98
    • 3 Nephi 9, 17, 18, 19, 22, 27, 28
    • Ether 3, 4, 12
    • Moses 6; Alma 5; 1 Nephi 17; Moroni 7 & 8; Mosiah 26

“Be still, and know.”
